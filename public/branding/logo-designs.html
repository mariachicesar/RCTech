Logo Usage Guidelines

  • Minimum Size: Logo should never be smaller than 1 inch in width for print, 120px for digital
  • Clear Space: Maintain clear space equal to the height of "RC" around all sides
  • Colors: Always use official brand colors - never alter or approximate
  • Background: Ensure sufficient contrast - use white version on dark backgrounds
  • Proportions: Never stretch, skew, or alter the proportions of the logo
  • Favicon: Use 16x16, 32x32, and 64x64 pixel versions for web
